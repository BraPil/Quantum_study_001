## Core Insight

> **AI decides what is happening. Quantum decides which combination of responses is mathematically best.**

These are different jobs. The classical hot path handles real-time threat response in seconds. The quantum cold path asks, in batch: "Could there have been a better policy?" — and answers it via QUBO optimization.
